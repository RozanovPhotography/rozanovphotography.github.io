How to install APK

  • 1. Download the apk file using your browser.

     

     

  • 2. Click "Open".

     

     

  • 3. Click "Settings".

     

     

  • 4. Turn on "Allow from this source". This message may vary depending on the device.

     

     

  • 5. Click "Accept". This message may vary depending on the device.

     

     

  • 6. Make sure "Allow from this source" is turned on. This message may vary depending on the device.

     

     

  • 7. Go back and click "Install".

     

     

  • 8. Installing...

     

     

  • 9. The app is successfully installed. Enjoy.
